How Mining Works
⛏ Proof of Work
Your device solves a SHA hash challenge. The NUC boss node verifies the result and rewards you with ARC.
💰 Rewards
0.25 ARC per block mined. Minodes (miner + node) earn 0.75 ARC. Halving of 20% every 10 months.
🔒 Thread Limit
Your trust score defines your base thread limit. Buy extra packs with ARC to unlock more mining power.
📈 Trust = Power
50 trust → 10 threads · 100 trust → 25 threads · 150 trust → 50 threads. Grow your reputation to mine more.
